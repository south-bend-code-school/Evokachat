EVOKAchat is created and designed by 5 ladies in code school. The goal of EVOKAchat is to help people through their problems by encouragement and connecting the person with other people with similar problems who will help them through it. Through this app you can fill out a form, so then you can send or request a note or card. The note or card can be used through schools, clubs or groups. The required age is 11 and up.
